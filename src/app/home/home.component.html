About Systers

Systers is an international Anita B.org community encouraging the participation of all women involved in technology. We are committed to providing a friendly, safe, and welcoming environment for all, regardless of gender, sexual orientation, ability, ethnicity, socioeconomic status, work experience, or religion (or lack thereof). We believe in inclusivity, diversity, and transparency. We value open source citizenship and collaborative involvement.
